Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2025

Tide ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal notes and diary application. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — your email address, used to create and authenticate your account via Supabase.
  • Notes and content — the text, images, tags, and other content you create inside Tide.
  • Device information — basic device and platform type (iOS, Android, web) used for app functionality, not for tracking.

We do not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, or behavioral tracking data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To sync your notes across your devices.
  • To authenticate you and protect your account.
  • To provide core app features (search, reminders, tags).

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a PostgreSQL-based cloud platform. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit (TLS). On mobile, a local SQLite copy is kept on your device for offline access.

4. Images

Images you attach to notes are uploaded to and stored in Supabase Storage. They are associated with your account and are not publicly accessible without authentication.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete individual notes or your entire account at any time. Upon account deletion, all associated data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access or export a copy of your data.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Delete your account and all associated data.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

Tide is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Request Account & Data Deletion

You may request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated data (notes, images, tags, and account information) at any time. We will process your request within 30 days.
